A fake footer is a footer that can be changed as needed. For example, you can use a fake paragraph above the footer to prevent page content from being scrolled behind the footer. You can make a footer invisible by using the hidden attribute. You can specify either 'hidden' (without value) or 'hidden="hidden"'. A hidden footer is not visible, but maintains its position on the page. An effective footer should include essential information, such as: Contact details, Navigation links, Legal information.
